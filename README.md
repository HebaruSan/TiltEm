# TiltEm

*This mod adds axial tilt on planets for [Kerbal Space Program (KSP)](https://kerbalspaceprogram.com)*

PlanetTilt.cfg reference:

Sun - 0
Kerbin - 1
Mun - 2
Minmus - 3
Moho - 4
Eve - 5
Duna - 6
Ike - 7
Jool - 8
Laythe - 9
Vall - 10
Bop - 11
Tylo - 12
Gilly - 13
Pol - 14
Dres - 15
Eeloo - 16
